## App Description

Pinochle Pal is the ultimate companion for your card game nights. Designed specifically for Pinochle enthusiasts, this elegant app eliminates the need for paper scorecards and mental math, letting you focus on what matters most—playing the game you love.

**GAME MODES FOR EVERY TABLE**

Whether you're playing head-to-head or hosting a full table, Pinochle Pal has you covered:

• 2-Handed Pinochle - Perfect for one-on-one competition
• 3-Handed Pinochle - Cutthroat style with individual scoring
• 4-Handed Partnership - Full team play with automatic team score tracking

**VISUAL MELD CALCULATOR**

Stop memorizing point values! Our intuitive meld calculator makes scoring effortless:

• Tap card combinations to instantly calculate meld points
• Visual display of all standard melds including Run, Marriage, Pinochle, and Around combinations
• Support for single and double melds
• Never miss or miscalculate a meld again

**SMART SCORE VALIDATION**

Built-in intelligence catches scoring mistakes before they happen:

• Automatic trick point validation (ensures points add up correctly)
• Warnings for unusual or impossible meld combinations
• Set detection when bidders fail to make their contract
• Clear visual feedback for every scoring decision

**COMPLETE GAME TRACKING**

Every hand matters, and Pinochle Pal remembers them all:

• Full round-by-round history with bid amounts and trump suits
• Running score totals updated in real-time
• Player statistics across all your games
• Game history to review past matches

**ANALYTICS DASHBOARD**

Understand your game like never before:

• Points composition breakdown (meld vs. tricks)
• Bidding performance tracking
• Win/loss statistics per player
• Visual charts for easy analysis

**DESIGNED FOR YOUR PHONE**

Pinochle Pal is built from the ground up for mobile:

• Clean, intuitive touch interface
• Beautiful card table aesthetic with rich green felt design
• Works perfectly offline—no internet required
• All data stays private on your device
• Fast and responsive on all iPhone models

**PRIVACY FIRST**

Your game data never leaves your device. No accounts required, no data collection, no ads. Just pure Pinochle scoring.
